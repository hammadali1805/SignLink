Empowering Communities with Sign Language Accessibility

As per the data collected from WHO about 5% of the total world population which constitute to be 360 million suffer from hearing loss and probably they can suffer muteness too and number of people who know sign language are decreasing day by day, we are not so far from the day when no one will know sign language except the one who needs it the most. So to counter this problem we are aiming to create such an app which will make everyone familiar to sign language.
